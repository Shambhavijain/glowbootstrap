"The Genesis of Gleam & Glow" It’ll blow your mind.

Every great story begins with a dream. For us, it started with a simple yet profound passion: to create perfumes that are more than just scents but are experiences that linger in the hearts and minds of those who encounter them. Born out of a love for the art of perfumery and a desire to craft unique olfactory experiences, Gleam & Glow embarked on a journey to transform this dream into reality. Discover Kannauj’s rose water, renowned for its pure, handpicked roses and traditional distillation. Each drop embodies the city’s rich perfume heritage, offering a delicate, soothing aroma that reflects centuries of artisanal expertise.

"The Timeless Art of Perfumery: " Kannauj’s Rich Legacy

The production of attar in Kannauj is a meticulous and artisanal process. Skilled craftsmen use age-old techniques to create perfumes by blending various natural ingredients. The art of creating attar involves a deep understanding of fragrance profiles and the skillful balancing of ingredients.
